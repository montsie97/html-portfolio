Best Fall Movies 2023

If you're looking for spooky films you found the place to be!

Howl's Moving Castle

This is a movie that does the book version wonders. From the detailed animation to the storyline, it is an absolute masterpiece and worth your time to watch.

Halloweentown

Who could forget this renown Disney classic? Marney's adventures, specifically those with her grandmother make for a spooky heartwarming experience.

Practical Magic

Starring Sandra Bullock and Nicole Kidman, this film explores the bond of sisterhood and the strength needed to battle forces much larger than they seem. The magic throughout the film takes you for a whirlwind of a plot.
